NBA Quiz - Easy Level - Answers

1. Who is known as "The King" and currently plays for the Los Angeles Lakers?

Answer: LeBron James

2. Which team does Stephen Curry play for in the NBA?

Answer: Golden State Warriors

3. Name one NBA team based in Texas.

Answer: Houston Rockets (or Dallas Mavericks, San Antonio Spurs)

4. The NBA Finals is a best-of-seven series. (True/False)

Answer: True

5. Michael Jordan played his entire NBA career with the Chicago Bulls. (True/False)

Answer: False

6. Who is the all-time leading scorer in NBA history?

Answer: Kareem Abdul-Jabbar

7. In which year did the NBA introduce the three-point line?

Answer: 1986
